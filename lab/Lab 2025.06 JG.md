5. Transformation protocol
	1. **Thaw competent cells** - Remove DH5α cells from -80°C and thaw on ice for 10-15 minutes
	2. **Add DNA** - Gently add 1-5 µL of plasmid DNA with 50-100 µL of competent cells
	    1. Use minimal pipetting to avoid damaging cells
	    2. Incubate on ice for 20-30 minutes
	3. **Heat shock** - Transfer tubes to 42°C water bath for exactly **45 seconds**
	    1. Critical timing - don't exceed 45-50 seconds
	4. **Ice recovery** - Immediately return to ice for 2-5 minutes
	5. **Add SOC** - Add 250-500 µL of room temperature SOC medium
	    1. Don't add SOC if plating on non-antibiotic plates
	6. **Outgrowth** - Incubate at 37°C with shaking (200-250 rpm) for 45-60 minutes
	    1. Skip this step for ampicillin selections (can plate immediately)
	7. **Plate** - Spread 50-200 µL on selective plates and incubate overnight at 37°C
